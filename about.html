Company Background

Multinational experience, independent shop.

We're a newly established company backed by decades of leadership exzations. Our founder perience in multinational organihas held key operations, supply chain, and engineering roles at Universal Robina Corporation, Puratos Philippines, Newly Weds Foods, Isuzu AutoParts, Nidec Philippines, and James Hardie Philippines.

That background shows up in how we work: Lean Manufacturing, Kaizen, FSSC certification, and hygiene-engineering discipline, applied to food manufacturing set-ups, industrial projects, and everyday engineering service — scaled down to work for a single line, not just a whole plant.

We bridge the gap between mechanical systems and electronic controls, helping manufacturers optimize productivity, ensure machine reliability, and adopt automation strategies they can actually afford.
